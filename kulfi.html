Kesar Pista Kulfi

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 litre full cream milk
  • 1/2 cup chopped pistachios
  • 2 tablespoon milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 10 strand saffron
  • 1/2 teaspoon powdered green cardamom

Instructions

  1. In a pan, add full cream milk and let it come to a boil. Once done, let it simmer for about 25-30 mins. Stir occasionally. Meanwhile, soak saffron strands in 2 tablespoon warm milk.
  2. Now, add sugar to milk and mix it well. Let it simmer till the milk thickens and then add pistachios, cardamom powder, and soaked saffron milk. Mix everything well and let it simmer again for 5 mins. Switch off the gas flame and let the milk cool for a while.
  3. Pour the mixture into kulfi moulds once it has thickened well. Freeze it for at least 4-5 hours.
  4. Once done, insert a wooden stick inside the kulfi mould and gently take the kulfi out. Top it with chopped pistachios and saffron strands. Your Kesar Pista Kulfi is ready. Enjoy.
