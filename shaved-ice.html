THE ICE CREAM STORE

Shaved Ice and Snoballs

Ingredients

  • 8 cups ice cubes
  • 12 tbsp snow cone syrup

Instructions

  1. Shave the ice cubes into a fluffy snow texture.
  2. Fill 6 oz foam cups or containers of your choice with the shaved ice. Drizzle with 2 tablespoons of snow cone syrup.
  3. Top with a ball of shaved ice. Drizzle with 1 tablespoon of syrup.
  4. Add any additional toppings of choice. Serve immediately.
