Strawberry Gelato

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh strawberries
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup whole milk (water, 2% or skim milk can be used)
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • ½ cup heavy whipping cream

Instructions

  1. Remove the stems and leaves from the strawberries and rinse in cold water.
  2. Place the strawberries, lemon juice and the sugar in a food processor and process until liquefied.
  3. Add the milk and run the food processor until all the mixture is smooth.
  4. Whip the cream with a wire whisk until it begins to thicken slightly and becomes the consistency of buttermilk.
  5. Mix the cream and strawberry mixture in a bowl and mix thoroughly.
  6. Pour the mixture into a gelato or ice cream maker and freeze following the manufacturer's instructions.
  7. Place the finished gelato into a freezer safe container and allow to fully freeze before serving (2-3 hours).
