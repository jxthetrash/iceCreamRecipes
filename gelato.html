THE ICE CREAM STORE

Strawberry Gelato

Ingredients

Instructions

1 pound fresh strawberries 1 cup granulated sugar ¾ cup whole milk (water, 2% or skim milk can be used) 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice ½ cup heavy whipping cream

Remove the stems and leaves from the strawberries and rinse in cold water. Place the strawberries, lemon juice and the sugar in a food processor and process until liquefied. Add the milk and run the food processor until all the mixture is smooth. Whip the cream with a wire whisk until it begins to thicken slightly and becomes the consistency of buttermilk. Mix the cream and strawberry mixture in a bowl and mix thoroughly. Pour the mixture into a gelato or ice cream maker and freeze following the manufacturer’s instructions. Place the finished gelato into a freezer safe container and allow to fully freeze before serving (2-3 hours).
