THE ICE CREAM STORE

Falooda

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup of basil seeds (takmania)
  • Falooda sev noodles, broken into 8 cm lengths
  • 1 cup of rose syrup (rooh afzah), or to taste, plus extra, to serve
  • Scoops vanilla ice-cream or kulfi
  • 2 cups of cold milk
  • Basil sprigs, to serve

Instructions

  1. Place basil seeds in a bowl and cover well with water. Soak for 2 hours, then strain through a fine sieve or muslin-lined bowl.
  2. Meanwhile, cook noodles in a small saucepan of boiling water for 3 minutes or until tender. Drain, refresh, then drain again.
  3. Divide noodles among serving glasses. Evenly spoon over rose syrup, then reserved basil seeds. Place 2 scoops of ice-cream in each glass and pour over milk. Drizzle over extra rose syrup, then top with a basil sprig and serve immediately.

Note

  • Basil seeds (takmania), falooda sev noodles and rose syrup (rooh afzah) are from Indian and Middle Eastern food shops.
